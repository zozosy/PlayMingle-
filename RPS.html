Rock Paper Scissor

Objective

The objective of Rock, Paper, Scissors is to win a series of quick rounds by choosing one of three hand signs (rock, paper, or scissors) that can defeat your opponent's choice based on simple rules

How to play

  • Choose a signal (e.g., "Rock, Paper, Scissors, Shoot!").
  • Both players form one of three hand signs simultaneously: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  • Determine the round winner based on the rules
  • Play multiple rounds, typically best out of three.
  • The player who wins the majority of rounds is the overall winner.
