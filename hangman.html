Objective

Hangman is a word-guessing game that is typically played with two or more players.

How to play

  • One player thinks of a word and draws blank spaces for each letter.
  • Other players guess one letter at a time.
  • If a guessed letter is in the word, it's filled in; if not, a part of the hangman is drawn
  • The game continues until the word is guessed or the hangman is completed.
